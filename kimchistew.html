Kimchi Stew

category : Soup Quick

Kimchi Jjigae is a classic dish in Korean cuisine, which is often referred to as the ultimate comfort food for Koreans around the world. It is savory and spicy and super easy to make.

prep time

10 mins.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Place the kimchi and kimchi brine in a shallow pot. Add pork and onion. Slice 2 green onions diagonally and add them as well.

step 2

Add salt, sugar, hot pepper flakes, and hot pepper paste. Drizzle sesame oil over top and add the anchovy stock.

step 3

Cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium high heat. After 10 minutes, add tofu and season to taste. then cover and cook another 10-15 minutes.

ingredients

1 lb kimchi, cut into bite size pieces

¼ cup kimchi brine

½ pound pork belly

½ package of tofu

1 medium onions

1 tsp kosher salt

2 tsp sugar

2 tsp korean red pepper flakes

1 tbsp gochujang

1 tsp toasted sesame oil

2 cups anchovy stock
