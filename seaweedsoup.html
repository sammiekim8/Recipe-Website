Seaweed Soup

category : soup

Miyeok-guk or seaweed soup is a seaweed based soup with beef brisket. It is a soup most commonly enjoyed on your birthday. It is also given to women after giving birth because the soup is very rich in iron and other nutrients.

prep time

40 mins.

cook time

60 mins.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Soak the seaweed for 30 minutes then rinse and drain. Make sure to squeeze out excess water. Cut into bite-sized pieces about 2 inches.

step 2

Add seaweed and 8 cups of water and bring to a boil on high heat. Takes about 10-12 minutes.

step 3

Turn dow heat to medium and add the chopped meat. Cover and let cook for 40 minutes.

step 4

Add garlic and let it cook on medium heat for another 10 minutes.

step 5

Finally, stir in sesame oil and serve.
