Dalgona

category : Quick Dessert

Light and crunchy with a toasty caramel like taste. This snack is commonly sold by vendors outside schools or toy stores. A shape would be stamped on and if you managed to poke it out with a needle, the vendor would offer another one for free!

prep time

5 mins.

cook time

5 mins.

serving

1 servings

instructions

step 1

The candy sets quickly, so have everything ready: Set the baking soda, a sheet of parchment paper, a small skillet and a 3-inch metal cookie cutter (any shape) on a heatproof work surface next to the stove. Rub a thin sheen of oil on half of the paper.

step 2

Place the sugar in a metal ladle and gently wave over a medium-low flame on a gas stove until the sugar melts all around the edges, 2 to 3 minutes. Slowly and continuously stir the sugar with a wooden or metal chopstick until any lumps have dissolved, 1 to 2 minutes. You want the sugar to melt very slowly and not darken past gold, so move the ladle away from the heat or lower the heat as needed.

step 3

Turn off the heat and add the baking soda. Quickly stir until evenly incorporated, then immediately pour onto the ungreased side of the parchment in a round. Fold the oiled side over the round, flatten with the skillet, uncover and press the cookie cutter in the center. Cool completely.

ingredients

A pinch of baking soda

Vegetable oil, for greasing

1 heaping tablespoon granulated sugar
