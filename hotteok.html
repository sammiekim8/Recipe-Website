Korean Sweet Pancake

category : Dessert

Hotteok or Korean sweet pancake is a crispy and chewy pancake filled with brown sugar and walnuts. It is a popular street snacks in Korea.

prep time

60 mins.

cook time

20 mins.

serving

8 servings

instructions

step 1

Add 1 cup of lukewarm water into a mixing bowl then mix in white sugar, yeast, kosher salt, vegetable oil, and stir it well. Then add 2 cups all purpose flour and mix until combined, do not over mix.

step 2

Cover the dough and let rise at room temperateure for 60 mins. Once the dough has doubled in size, knead the air out and let it rise another 20 minutes.

step 3

For the filling: Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and walnuts in a small bowl.

step 4

Spread 1/2 cup of flour onto a cutting board and knead the dough. Seperate dough into 8 equal sized pieces. Flatten the dough, add a spoonful of the filling, seal the filling and roll the dough into a ball.

step 5

On medium heat, add tbsp of oil and fry the dough balls for 30 secs then flip and flatten with your spatula and let cook another 30 secs.

ingredients

1 cup water

2 tbsp white sugar

2 tsp dry yeast

½ tsp kosher salt

1 tbsp neautral oil

2½ cups AP flour (set ½ cup aside for later)


Filling

½ cup turbinado sugar (or brown sugar)

1 tsp cinnamon powder

2 tbsp chopped walnuts
