Steamed eggs

category : Quick

Gyeranjjim or steamed eggs is a Korean savory egg custard dish. It is a popular side dish for breakfast, lunch, and dinner both at home and restaurants. You can make it simply with eggs and scallions, but other chopped vegetables such as carrot, onion, and/or zucchini are great additions as well.

prep time

5 mins.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Beat the egg in a bowl and run trhough a sieve to create a smooth consistency.

step 2

Mix the beaten eggs well with the anchovy broth and salt.

step 3

Lightly oil your pot or bowl with 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil or cooking oil.

step 4

Place the bowl with the egg mixture in the microwave. Don't need to cover it. Cook for 2 minutes, and stir. Cook for another 2 minutes, and stir. Check to see if the egg is still runny in the middle, and cook for another minute or until the egg is set in the middle.
