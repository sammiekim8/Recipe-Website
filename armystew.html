Army Stew

category : Soup Quick

After the Korean war, food was scarce so Koreans had to utilize food supplies left behind by U.S. soldiers like spam, sausages, and sliced american cheese. Using those ingredients combined with Korean ingredients like kimchi, chili paste, Budae jigae or Army Stew was created. It is still very popular today and has become a staple in Korean cuisine.

prep time

15 mins.

cook time

15 mins.

serving

8 servings

instructions

step 1

Add all ingredient into a pot, cover and bring to a boil, about 10 minutes.

step 2

When the stew starts to boil, reduce the heat to medium and cook for about 5 minutes. (Optional) Add the noodles and cheese, and boil for another 3-4 minutes until the noodles are cooked through.

ingredients

½ can spam

4 oz sausages

½ onion

1 cup water

3 cups anchovy stock

1 cup kimchi

2 tbsp red pepper flakes

1 tbsp chili paste

1 tbsp mirin

1 pack of ramen (optional)

1 ts cinnamon powder 2 tbs chopped walnuts
