Spicy Rice Cakes

category : Entree

Tteokbokki or spicy rice cakes is a popular korean street food. The rice cakes are soft and chewy and is covered in a sweet and spicy sauce.

prep time

20 mins.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Soak rice cakes in warm water for 10 minutes or until tender.

step 2

Boil the soup stock in a shallow pot over medium high heat and dissolve the tteokbokki sauce. Once the seasoned stock is boiling, add the rice cakes, fish cakes and onion. Boil them a further 3 to 5 mins until the rice cakes are fully cooked.

step 3

Then lower the heat and simmer until the sauce thicken.

step 4

Add the sesame oil, sesame seeds, and green onion then quickly stir. Serve warm.

ingredients

1 lb rice cakes

1 cup fish cakes

2 tsp toasted sesame oil

1 tbsp sesame seeds

2 stalks scallions


Sauce

3 cups anchovy stock

3 tbsp gochujang

1 ½ tbsp sugar

1 ½ tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp Korean red pepper flakes

2 cloves garlic minced
